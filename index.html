Hello! I'm Andrew.

I'm a seasoned software engineer and educator seeking developer advocate opportunities where I can help others grow while learning new things and collaborating to tackle difficult problems. With experience spanning web development bootcamp instruction, senior software engineering, and community building, I bring a unique perspective to developer relations.

My approach combines technical depth with genuine human connection. I've taught 500+ students from zero coding background to shipping full-stack applications. I've written production code in Python, JavaScript, React, TypeScript, and Rust. I've given dozens of public talks through Toastmasters and technical conferences. Most importantly, I believe in meeting developers where they are and helping them reach where they want to be.

Beyond pure technology, I'm passionate about creating inclusive communities where everyone can contribute. Whether through running Dungeons & Dragons games for new players, organizing public speaking events, or mentoring junior developers, I focus on lowering barriers and amplifying voices.
